Short Reflection

Working across a gallery, logo projects, and a public campaign changed how I think about art. I learned that design isn’t only about “taste”—it’s a conversation between message, audience, and timing. At Corner Gallery, I saw how a wall label can steer a viewer’s pace; with BEOK, I learned to defend choices with systems (color, typography, grid); in the campaign work, I practiced listening to constraints and still making something kind and clear.
