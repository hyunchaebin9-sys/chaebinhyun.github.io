Spring 2024 Environmental Group Exhibition

“Art is not only a mirror — it is a gesture of care.”

Role: Participating Artist · Duration: April 2024 (4 exhibitions)

As part of the ACT Spring Arts Festival, I joined a group of student artists to create and exhibit artwork centered on environmental themes. From planning and installation to visitor engagement, I was actively involved in all aspects of the exhibition process.

The event ran as a local cultural series held four times, promoted through community posters and word of mouth. All proceeds from artwork sales were donated to the wildfire relief fund for the Gyeongbuk wildfire (March 2025) via ACT and affiliated NGOs.

This project was both a platform for creative expression and a meaningful act of solidarity — delivering urgent environmental messages through art while contributing to community recovery and awareness.
