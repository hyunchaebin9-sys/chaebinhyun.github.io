About Me

Hello! I'm Chaebin, a high school student based in Seoul, South Korea. I’m passionate about fine arts, digital design, and creative storytelling that connects emotion with social awareness.

Through my art, I explore how color, material, and form can express aging, empathy, and collective memory. I aim to continue combining visual art and technology to communicate ideas that inspire reflection and care.
